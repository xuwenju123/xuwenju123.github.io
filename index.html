Bio

I am a Staff Research Engineer at OPPO US research Center at Palo Alto, CA. Previously, I worked as a Research Scientist in JD Tech R&D Center at Silicon Valley. I finished my Ph.D. at the University of Kansas. During my Ph.D. study, I worked at Philips Research and MERL as a research intern in 2018 and 2019, respectively. My research interest lies in computer vision and deep learning, including image synthesis, object recognition/detection/segmentation, and camera localization. My works have appeared in TOG, TIP, TMM, ICCV, ACM MM. I serve as a reviewer for more than 15 top international journals and conferences.
